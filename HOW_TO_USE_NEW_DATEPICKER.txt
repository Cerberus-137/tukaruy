CONTOH:

"Ship Date Range: Jul 2 - Jul 10"

Tracking list sudah difilter dengan range tsb.

================================================================================
                      CONTOH REAL
================================================================================

USER INGIN: Filter tracking numbers yang ship tanggal 2-10 Juli

LANGKAH:

1. Click "Select date range..."
   вң“ Modal opens

2. Click From Date field вҶ’ Pilih tanggal 2
   вң“ Input shows: 2026-07-02

3. Click To Date field вҶ’ Pilih tanggal 10
   вң“ Input shows: 2026-07-10
   вң“ Display shows: "Selected: Jul 2 - Jul 10"

4. Click "Apply Date Range"
   вң“ Modal closes
   вң“ Sidebar shows: "Ship Date Range: Jul 2 - Jul 10"
   вң“ Tracking list shows only items with ship date 2-10 Juli

DONE! вң…

                      VALIDATION
================================================================================

KALAU USER INPUTNYA SALAH:

Scenario 1: Start date SETELAH end date
в”Җв”Җв”Җв”Җв”Җв”Җв”Җв”Җв”Җв”Җв”Җв”Җв”Җв”Җв”Җв”Җв”Җв”Җв”Җв”Җв”Җв”Җв”Җв”Җв”Җв”Җв”Җв”Җв”Җв”Җв”Җв”Җв”Җв”Җв”Җв”Җв”Җв”Җв”Җв”Җв”Җ

- From Date: 10 Juli
- To Date: 2 Juli

Kalau click Apply:
вқҢ Error: "Start date must be before end date"

User harus pilih lagi dengan urutan benar.


Scenario 2: Tidak ada salah satu date
в”Җв”Җв”Җв”Җв”Җв”Җв”Җв”Җв”Җв”Җв”Җв”Җв”Җв”Җв”Җв”Җв”Җв”Җв”Җв”Җв”Җв”Җв”Җв”Җв”Җв”Җв”Җв”Җв”Җв”Җв”Җв”Җв”Җв”Җв”Җв”Җв”Җв”Җв”Җв”Җв”Җ
User hanya pilih From Date, tidak pilih To Date

Kalau click Apply:
вқҢ Error: "Please select both start and end dates"

User harus isi kedua fields.
